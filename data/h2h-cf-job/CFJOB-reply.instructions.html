The Task

Imagine that you are playing a game where you can play either as a hotline operator , or a client which tries to find a restaurant for tonight in Cambridge. The client is given a predefined goal to find information about a specific kind of restaurant they want to visit. The hotline operator tries to help the client to find a restaurant which satisfies client's requirements.

The rules of game are that after you write a reply either as a client or as operator you pass the conversation to other players and you pick a random conversation from other players for the next round. In this CrowdFlower job you provide a reply and in the next round you are assigned a new conversation and role (either operator or client).

The goal of the game is to create conversations which satisfy client's goals, are natural, polite and are relevant to the topic.
