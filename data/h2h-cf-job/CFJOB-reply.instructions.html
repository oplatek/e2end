The Task

Imagine you are playing a game where you can play either a hotline operator or client which tries to find a restaurant for tonight in Cambridge. The client has given a predefined goal what kind of restaurant he want to visit. The hotline operator tries to help the client to find one of the restaurant which satisfies client requirements.

The rules of games are that after you write a reply either as a client or as operator you pass the conversation to other players and you pick random conversation from other players for next round. In this task's implementation you provide a reply and in the next round you are assigned a new conversation and role (either operator or client).

The goal of the game is to create conversations which satisfy client goals, are natural, polite and are achieved in shortest number of rounds.
