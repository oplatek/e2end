The Task

Imagine that you are either a a hotline operator , or a client which tries to find a restaurant for tonight in Cambridge. The clients are given predefined goals to find information about specific kind of restaurants they want to visit. The hotline operators try to help the clients to find a restaurants which satisfy clients' requirements.

The rules of game are that after you write a reply either as a client or as operator you pass the conversation to other players and you pick a random conversation from other players for the next round. In this CrowdFlower job you provide a reply and in the next round you are assigned a new conversation and role (either operator or client).

Try to create conversations which satisfy client's goals, are natural, polite and are relevant to the topic.
